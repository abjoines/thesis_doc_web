PROTOTYPE 1

Animating the Transition Between Homeomorphic Surfaces

After identifying the subject matter for my thesis, the first prototype was dedicated to exploring pre-existing tools that could be used in the creation of homeomorphic surfaces. Since the rules of homeomorphisms state that there can be no cutting, puncturing, or gluing I decided to create a couple of animations to show the seamless transition from one surface to another. Using Cinema 4D I deformed both a cube and a sphere with built-in deformers that allowed for animation. The results of these experiments are below.
