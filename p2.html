PROTOTYPE 2

Generating Homeomorphic Sets

For the second prototype, I decided to take a programmatic approach to the generation of homeomorphisms. I chose to use sets of points for this prototype in order to explore this subject through a two-dimensional lens. that is not to say that these points could not also move along the z-axis. To create this prototype I used p5.js, forming a grid of homeomorphic sets in order to convey the many possible ways these points could be organized. To maintain clarity, the central node of each set is anchored in the middle. I also wanted to use this prototype to experiment with interaction.
