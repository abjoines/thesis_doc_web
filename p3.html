Digital Kinetic Sculpture: The Cornu Spiral

In the previous prototypes, the generation of homeomorphisms hadnâ€™t been controlled. What I mean by this is that there was no end result or surface in mind when deforming the sphere or the cube and that the sets of points were randomly generated. For this prototype, I wanted to deform a surface with intention. The first step in this was determining what I wanted the target surface to be. I chose the Euler Spiral, also known as the Cornu Spiral because I figured a curve would be a good place to start with this kind of deformation.
